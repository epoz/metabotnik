Create huge zoomable images

Try zooming in & out, click and drag.

How does it work?

  • Use your Dropbox account to log in.
  • A folder called /Apps/Metabotnik is now created in your Dropbox
  • Make a folder in your /Apps/Metabotnik Dropbox
  • Fill it with images - take care that they have the order you want
  • Click the Start button below

Your zoomable file is now being created
Wait for the mail that tells you the work is done
