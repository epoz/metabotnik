An easy way to create huge zoomable images composed of hundreds of smaller images.

Here are some examples, try zooming in & out and clicking-and-dragging with your mouse.

Some pages of beautiful books through the centuries

A selection of historiated intitials

How does it work?

  • Log in to this site using your Dropbox account
  • Create a folder in your Dropbox and fill it with images
  • Choose the folder in your Dropbox to make a metabotnik
  • Arrange the sorting and layout of the images
  • Add metadata to the images and layers
  • Click the Generate button and wait for your gigantic zoomable image to be done
  • Share and enjoy your work!
